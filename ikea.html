IKEA asked us to create a job fair booth to attract new talent.
We created an immersive experience with IKEA’s values, simplicity and togetherness, in mind. Leaving the visitor with an itch to find out more rather than a soon-to-be-binned paper flyer.
We made it affordable, sustainable and scalable by using existing IKEA products and endless possibilities with the AR.
We called it: TALANGFÄLLAN
